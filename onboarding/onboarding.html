Welcome.

GOG Enhancer adds the bits the store doesn't: live currency conversion, price history, custom tags, Hebrew, and a bunch of small visual upgrades. Two minutes to set up.

First, your currency

We'll show every price in this currency next to the original USD.

Tax estimator

GOG shows USD without sales tax. We can show you the post-tax price too, based on a region preset. Pick what fits, or skip.

Choose your toggles

Everything is on by default. Untick anything you don't want — you can change all of these later from the popup.

Pick a look

Sets the accent colors across badges, the game-page panel, and the era-aware card effects. Change anytime in Advanced Options.

✓

You're set.

GOG Enhancer is active. Visit gog.com to see it in action.

  • Alt+G — open settings popup
  • Alt+Shift+G — toggle on/off
  • Alt+Shift+H — toggle Hebrew

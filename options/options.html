Privacy & transparency

GOG Enhancer talks to two domains and that's it:

  • www.gog.com — to read what's on the page and inject UI.
  • api.frankfurter.app — to fetch USD→EUR/ILS/GBP/PLN/RUB rates (no key, no tracking).

No analytics. No third-party fonts. Tags & notes never leave your machine.
